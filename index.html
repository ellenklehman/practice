About us

Started by two history teachers with passions for baking, A Time for Sweets opened in 2016. Previous students have helped develop ideas for the various confectionaries produced here.

  • Washington's Cherry Pie
  • Lexington and Concord Grape Jam Tart
  • Let Them Eat Chocolate Cake
